about
I started out making music in 2013 with the help of a friend who taught me the basics. Shortly after I was approached to produce records and albums for some local artists. I became a self taught musician, mixer/masterer, and engineer out of necessity and put out an instrumental tape in 2019. My music is very synth heavy and ambient, often lush and spacey, and I try to keep it as boundless as possible.
